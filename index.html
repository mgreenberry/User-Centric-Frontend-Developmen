Helping Children Communicate

Communicating with British Sign Language

For very young children, older children and adults, we offer British Sign Language (BSL) lessons aimed at absolute begineers to intermediate students.

From the basics like the alphabet, numbers and simple vocabulary to more complex conversational skills such as describing your family, work, and hobbies and interests, we have exciting classes for you.

You will also learn some exciting and interesting methods that will help you to learn and apply what you are learning, such as learning to juggle!

Our Courses

All of our British Sign Language courses are available online. Our tutor led courses are presented on video conferencing software such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Skype, and others.

Alternatively, you can sign up to our pre-recorded courses which can be accessed through our sponser - Enabled Services.

All of our courses are aimed at the following age groups: -

  • Children up to 7 years old
  • Children up to 13 years old
  • Young adults up to 18 years old
  • Adults

Our Company

Happy Hands was established to provide BSL classes for young children and their families as there are very few classes and courses available for young children and their families. Most BSL courses are designed for adults and may not cover some of the signs that occur in everyday life.

Our tutors have over 25 years experience in education and have up to date Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) checks
