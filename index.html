Our Courses

Happy Hands offers 3 differnt types of British Sign Language courses online via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Skype or similar.

Our Young Children's courses are aimed at children upto 7 years old, our Older Children's courses are aimed at children and young adults upto 13 years old, and our Adult courses are aimed at teenagers and parents/guardians

About Us

We are all about education, from the very young to the elderly, we feel you are never too young or never too old to learn something new.

Our aim is to provide fun and engaging online and recorded lessons for all ages. We have over 20 years experience in education and British Sign Langauge.
