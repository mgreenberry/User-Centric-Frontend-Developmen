Our Tutors and Prices

Michael - Teaches Young Adults and Adult learners

Michael is our Lead Tutor and has over 25 years of experience in British Sign Language, having worked with both Deaf children and adults in a variety of settings. He has been involved with the National Deaf Children's Society for 10 years as a volunteer and has also taught sign langauge for over 5 years.

Michael specialises in teaching Level 1 British Sign Language to college students and adults and has sucessfully supported multiple students through Level 1 course.

Michael has trained in Advanced Teaching Methods and uses these as part of his lessons to enable learners to retain and use what they've been learning.

Michael is registered as a tutor with Signature.org and has a Level 4 BSL and a TEFL qualification

Teacher B - Teaches young children from 0 to 13 years old

Rebekah loves helping children to learn to communicate effectively through the use of sign language.

She has qualified as an Early Years Practitioner and holds the NNEB, NVQ 4 in Childcare and Education and a TEFL teaching qualification.

She has worked with children for over twenty five years in a variety of childcare settings, during which she worked with children who use sign language to communicate. She has also worked as an Educational Needs coordinator in one of her roles.

She now uses this experience to teach our younger learners the basic signs necessary to communicate with their friends at school and family members. These fun lessons are a great introduction to sign language and many have gone on to complete their Level 1 after this course has been completed.
