Our Courses and Lessons

Our Children's Courses

Children under 7

We feel that you are never too young to learn British Sign Language, so our Young Child lessons are aimed at young children up to 7 years old.

Younger children will really enjoy these interactive and fun lessons. Simple and clear, these lessons will introduce age appropriate grammer to young children.

These lessons cover topics needed everyday such as: -

  • Alphabet
  • Numbers
  • Food
  • Clothing
  • Animals

And so much more!

Teacher B teaching a very young child the vowels in British Sign Language

Children from 8 to 13 years old

Our Older Child lessons are aimed at children from 8 to 13 years old.

These lessons are slightly more advanced and cover more of the grammer and structure of British Sign Language.

The lessons cover similar topics to our Young Child courses with additional content such as: -

  • Emotions
  • Weather
  • Transport
  • Directions

And so much more!

Teacher B teaching a young child part of the alphabet in British Sign Language

Our Adult Courses

Young adults from 13 to 18 years old

At Enabled Services, our Young Adult lessons are aimed at teenagers from 13 years to 18 years old.

These lessons are a lot more advanced and go into more detail about the grammer, linguistics and structure of British Sign Language.

These lessons follow the Signature Level 1 course syllabus. This starts with An Introduction to British Sign Language (course code BSL101): -

  • Fingerspelling & Numbers
  • Greetings and Simple Signs
  • Asking and Giving Personal Information
  • Describing the Weather
  • Asking about Travel & Transport
  • Asking for and Giving Directions

You can gain a recognised Signature certificate on completion of each section

From Enabled Services - Michael teaching some simple signs in British Sign Language

Adult Courses

At Enabled Services, our Adult lessons are aimed at parents, guardians or anyone interested in learning British Sign Language.

These lessons are very advanced and cover the core grammer, linguistics and structure elements of British Sign Language.

The lessons cover similar topics to our Young Adult courses with additional content such as: -

  • Numbers 1 - 1,000
  • Making Conversation
  • Describing People, Animals and Objects
  • Describing Your Home, Room, Building or Area
  • Describing Interests and Hobbies
  • Placement, Referencing and Orientation

And so much more!
